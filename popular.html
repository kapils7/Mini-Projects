YouTube is a video viewing & sharing platform with a monthly active user base of nearly 2 billion. This almost a third of global population. The added benefit of sharing video and music is that the artists are also rewarded monetarily. This is done through a comprehensive ad placements and sponsorships.

The most subscribed music artist by far on the YouTube, Justin Bieber boasts of videos which have hundreds of millions of views(!!). Ed Sheeran is 3rd most subsrcibed artist on YouTube. The British guitarist-vocalist-singer broke into industry in 2010 and with his skills won over fans.

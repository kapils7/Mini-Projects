ZAYN is a former member of famous boy-band One Direction (1D). The band was formed after 5 individual contestants were all paired together by the judges of X-Factor UK in 2010. After ending of the show, the group was signed to a major music label. Thus began their journey of global fame and popularity. 1D released 4 albums in 5 years, all of them were very successfull. In 2015, ZAYN left 1D to pursue his solo career. His supposed reason was that he wanted to get time off from overwhelming stress of tours, concerts and constant eyeballs on his every movement.

In March 2016, ZAYN released his first solo album "Mind Of Mine". The album went to the top of music charts across several countries within a day of release. Since then he has released singles and covers. His latest album "Icarus Falls" was released in December 2018. The "double" album consisted of 27 songs and the total album duration nearly-hitting an hour and half mark. Although it wasn't as successfull as his 2016 venture, it was loved by his fans.

ZAYN has also posed for popular magazines like PAPER, GQ, ELLE etc. His most popular cover came in 2017 with his girlfriend, supermodel Gigi Hadid for VOGUE August Editon.
